## Module 12 Lesson

### Activity: Mapping stakeholder expectations

| Stakeholder         | Expectation                                                                                                                                                         | Action / strategy                                                                                   |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Developer           | Clear summary of whether the code that they've implemented is successful and if not, a bug report that they can start analysing to find the cause and fix.         | Provide testing debriefs or reports and well written bugs with relevant supporting data.            |
| PO                  | Whether the acceptance criteria have been met, whether any additional work is required (defects or new stories) and any qualitative feedback or new risks.         | Have debriefs or showcases to sign off on work. Alternatively proactively speak to PO about new work raised. |
| Engineering Manager | Testing is done.                                                                                                                                                    | Provide information on quality & testing such as retro feedback or key metrics.                     |
| VP of division      | Customers are happy and do not complain.                                                                                                                          | Ensure that we adhere to SLAs and have evidence of work completed.                                  |
| Customer            | Software has a good user experience and behaves as they would expect.                                                                                             | Use analytics and feedback forms to learn whether customers are liking and using the product.       |
